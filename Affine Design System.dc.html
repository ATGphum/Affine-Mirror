Design System

Blackpaper visual system  ·  Internal reference
The rendering rules behind every Affine surface. Black is the field, white is the theorem, gold is earned. Use these tokens verbatim — restraint is the design.
00 / Principle
BLACK  FIELD
WHITE  THESIS
GOLD  VALIDATION
Black is not mood — it is the field of unresolved possibility. White is not decoration — it is the measurement, text, and structure. Gold is not glitter — it marks validation, inheritance, and aligned survival.
01 / The Mark
Two marks, one identity. Λ is the signature; the full Affine mark is the monument.
Monument
The full mark. Use for the landing hero and major section openers, in full color. Minimum 96px — below that, use the Λ.
Lambda — Λ
The small mark. Use for favicons, nav, footer, and document sign-offs. Minimum 16px; thicken the stroke below 24px so it stays solid. Don't use it as a standalone logo on charts, dashboards, or UI — use the full mark there.
Lambda is the signature. The Affine mark is the monument.

07 / Kill Criteria
Reject the design if any of these are true.
01
It looks like a generic dark-mode AI page.
02
It reads as a luxury crypto brand, or uses gold as decoration.
03
It needs paragraphs of explanation to justify the first screen.
04
It hides the alignment mechanism, or cannot express inheritance and replacement.
05
It abandons plainness for spectacle.
