How we hold the line behind every Affine surface
00 / Thesis
Affine makes intelligence compound.

Distributed intelligence can produce many useful outputs while still failing to accumulate progress. Without evaluation, alignment, and inheritance, each participant becomes locally useful but globally fragmented.

Affine exists to prevent that fragmentation. It is the alignment layer that turns open intelligence into cumulative intelligence. Everything in this document exists to keep that idea legible — in the copy, the diagrams, the restraint, and the gold.

02 / What Affine Is
The mechanism through which distributed intelligence becomes cumulative — aligning independent capabilities through evaluation pressure, validation grammar, inheritance, and composition.
Here, alignment has a bounded meaning: a candidate satisfies the shared evaluation and validity conditions required to become part of the inherited frontier. It is not a claim that one benchmark proves every moral or general meaning of AI alignment.
It is
A mechanism for cumulative intelligence
An evaluation and validation grammar
A layer for inheritance and composition
A way to decide what becomes foundation
It is not
A single model
A research lab
A passive marketplace
A chatbot or social network
A generic AI protocol or dashboard skin
It answers
What was produced?
What was evaluated?
What improved, and what failed?
What should be inherited?
What should be replaced?
What composes with what?
What becomes the new frontier?

04 / Design Laws
Nine rules. When a choice is uncertain, these decide it.
01
Plainness is authority
Remove every element that does not clarify the protocol, mechanism, or thesis.
02
Proof before atmosphere
Mechanism must carry more weight than mood.
03
Gold has a declared channel
The mark uses identity gold; every other gold state must earn validation or inheritance.
04
The monument is unresolved multiplicity
Contain its spectrum to rare identity moments; never turn it into a decorative palette.
05
Text is infrastructure
Copy should be compressed, exact, and able to survive technical scrutiny.
06
Diagrams are proof
Every line, threshold, and state must explain behavior.
07
Kinship without imitation
Mirror Bittensor's restraint and structure, but give Affine its own semantic system.
08
Accessibility is rigor
Quiet must never mean unreadable, color-only, or motion-dependent.
09
No unwritten exceptions
When the system changes intentionally, update the canon.
